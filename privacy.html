Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 27, 2026

1. Who We Are

Sol-Fi Inc. operates solfi.live, a next-generation validator node statistics and metrics monitoring portal for the Solana network. Contact: support@solfi.live, 3920 S Congress Ave, Suite 204, Austin, TX 78704.

2. Information We Collect

We collect only the data needed to provide analytics services:

  • Account Data: Email address and username you provide when creating an account.
  • On-Chain Data: Public Solana wallet address you choose to connect. We cannot access private keys or seed phrases.
  • Usage Data: IP address, browser type, and pages viewed for site security and performance.

Important: Sol-Fi is non-custodial. We do not collect, store, or request private keys, seed phrases, KYC documents, or ID photos. We do not hold user funds.

3. How We Use Your Data

We use data solely to:

  • Provide validator APY data, node score rankings, and uptime metrics.
  • Maintain your account and respond to support requests.
  • Improve site security and prevent fraud.

4. Data Storage & Security

All account data is stored on encrypted Firebase/Firestore servers. We use industry-standard SSL/TLS encryption for data in transit. Session data is stored via essential cookies/localStorage to keep you logged in.

5. Cookies & Trackers

We use only essential cookies required for login and site function. We do not use advertising or third-party tracking cookies.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell, lease, or share your email, wallet address, or usage data with third parties for marketing. We may share data only to comply with legal obligations.

7. Data Retention & Deletion

Account data is deleted within 30 days of account closure upon request to support@solfi.live. Public on-chain data is never deleted because it is public.

8. Your Rights

You may request access, correction, or deletion of your account data at any time by emailing support@solfi.live.

9. Changes to This Policy

We will post updates on this page. Continued use means you accept changes.

10. Contact

For privacy questions: support@solfi.live
