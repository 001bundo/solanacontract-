Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 27, 2026. This Privacy Policy details our practices regarding user information collection, protection, and usage.

1. Information Collection

We collect limited, necessary data to run our decentralized delegation and yield tracking operations:

  • Account Details: Usernames, email addresses, and encrypted authentication hashes.
  • On-Chain Details: Configured withdrawal wallet keys and transaction signature hashes you submit.
  • KYC Documents: Verification details (names, country, ID numbers, document photos) uploaded strictly for compliance and auditing.

2. Data Storage & Security

All accounts, ledger balances, and KYC uploads are stored securely inside our Firebase Firestore database. The active session and locally generated keys are cached on your browser via localStorage for performance. We employ industry-standard encryption protocols (SSL/TLS) to secure data in transit.

3. KYC Data Retention & Privacy

KYC document scans are accessible only to authorized root auditor accounts. They are kept securely inside our server buckets and are deleted automatically if your account is closed or if verification fails and is rejected by the system.

4. Cookies & Trackers

We do not use tracking or advertising cookies. Our system only uses local cache variables (essential storage values) in your browser to maintain your sign-in state and preferences.

5. External Sharing

SolStaking does not share, lease, or sell user email addresses, balance ledgers, or document scans to third-party marketing companies, validator nodes, or external agencies.

6. Policy Modifications

We reserve the right to modify this policy. Any changes will be announced on this page. By continuing to use the platform, you acknowledge and accept our guidelines.
