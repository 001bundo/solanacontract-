2. Staking & Delegation Operations

Sol-Fi acts as an automated routing gateway. When you deposit SOL, USDT, or BTC, you choose to delegate those funds to be staked on active Solana validator nodes.

  • Staking Plans: Delegations are bound to plan rates (ROI percentages) and plan durations (days) as configured in the platform database.
  • Staking Rewards: Yield distributions accrue daily and are credited to your platform balance.
  • Contract Progress: When a plan's duration finishes, the contract status transitions to "In Progress" and awaits audit verification from portal administrators before being marked "Completed".

3. Deposits & Withdrawals

Deposits are credited only after submitting valid transaction signature hashes and uploading proof-of-payment receipts, which are audited by administrators. Withdrawals are verified and dispatched to your saved blockchain address in order of queue processing.
