Frequently Asked Questions

Have questions? We have compiled responses to the most frequent inquiries from investors.

What is Solana Contract?
Solana Contract is a decentralized yield staking dashboard that operates automated reward allocation algorithms on deposits. Yields are generated by staking assets on Solana validators and liquidity systems.
How do I purchase a contract?
Simply sign up, navigate to the Deposit section on the dashboard, transfer SOL or USDT to the platform deposit address, input your transaction hash, and use the approved balance to buy a contract plan.
Are withdrawals processed instantly?
Yes. Once a withdrawal is approved by administrative auditors, the smart system executes the transfer immediately to your configured SOL or USDT wallet.
What is the referral program structure?
You receive a direct referral credit of 5% on all contracts purchased by accounts registered using your affiliate link. Referral rewards are immediately withdrawable.
